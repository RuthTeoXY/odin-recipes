Tomato Egg

Description
The tomato egg dish is so simple to put together, yet so satisfying. It can be a nutritious one dish meal on it's own. Literally 10mins to put together! Let's get them eggs cracking!
Ingredients
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 riped tomato
  • 1 stalk of spring onion
  • chicken stock cubes
Steps
  1. Crack the eggs in a bowl and beat them well. Season with a light dash of soy sauce and pepper.
  2. Dice the tomatoes and salt them with a pinch of salt.
  3. In a hot pan, add 2 tablespoons of oil, then pour egg mixture in.
  4. Scramble the eggs until 70% cooked through. Set aside in a plate.
  5. Heat up 2 more tablespoons of oil and then add the tomatoes. Stir fry until soft.
  6. Return the eggs back into the pan with the tomatoes. Add 2 tablespoons of water and simmer over low heat.
  7. Season with salt and sugar. Garnish with spring onions and serve!
